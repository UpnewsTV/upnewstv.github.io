How it works?


Recognize customer and
automatically optimize campaigns according to past behavior

Whatever your business sector,
we have a solution that will fit your business.

Based on the historical data of digital signage parts special developed for your shop or restaurant, we analize past behavior and customer preferences (including third-party customer) to further personalize interactions.
It's easy

All parts of digital signage are based on mobile apps, which are install on tablets, Android TV, or cheap Android Stick connected with LCD-, PDP-panel. We get user’s pattern through iBeacon infrastructure.

upnews enables you to make better use of your existing TV’s

Demonstrate your content directly among goods on the shelves or resell the advertising space to third-party companies. Our apps install both: on small tablets and on huge PDP-panels.

It's cool

Make real news and entertaining tv channel with upnews! Share social media feeds from Facebook, Twitter, Instagram on your TV. Display news from RSS, weather forecast and exchange rates.

Personal Contact

Tell about you goods when your customer really needs it. Make personal offer based on customers preferences and their last purchases.

DIVERSE CONTENT

Your own or your customer’s video- and audio- content. Share social media feeds from Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and RSS.

Managing

Personal account, logical simple interface, iBeacon's infrastructure managing for tracking customers movement inside premises (shop or café, or bank).

Personification

Customer identification, past behavior analisys, customer preferences and purchases history. Special personal offers and discounts.

Report in details

Statistic of audio and video displays, counting persons looking each video, online control of hardware.
